About Publications

I am an Applied Scientist at Adobe Experience Platform (AEP) AI, conducting R&D at the intersection of HCI and ML, focused on advancing the reasoning and interaction infrastructure of the AEP Agent Orchestrator. I got my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of Notre Dame, advised by Prof. Toby Jia-Jun Li. Before joining ND, I received two M.S. degrees in Computer Science from the University of Rochester and the University of Minnesota, where I was advised by Dr. Zhen Bai and Dr. Haiyi Zhu respectively. I received B.Eng. in Software Engineering from Shaanxi Normal University. I have also worked at Apple, AWS AI and Meta Reality Labs as a research scientist intern.

My research focuses on human-AI interaction, aiming to design interactive AI-powered systems that provide adaptive, context-sensitive, on-demand support throughout complex cognitive tasks. I have developed human-AI collaborative tools that adapt dynamically to evolving user goals, providing progressive cognitive scaffolding to enhance social interaction and learning beyond the traditional classroom. Additionally, I have implemented semi-automated systems that learn incrementally from user demonstrations, enabling AI to gradually streamline workflows, accelerate insight generation, and support meaningful decision-making.
